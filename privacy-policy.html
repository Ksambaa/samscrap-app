Privacy Policy for Education Network Analysis

Last updated: February 10, 2025

1. Introduction

This privacy policy describes how Education Network Analysis ("we", "our", or "app") collects and processes your LinkedIn data for educational analysis purposes.

2. Data Collection

Our app collects the following information from your LinkedIn profile through the official LinkedIn API:

  • Education information from your connections
  • Basic profile information necessary for analysis

3. Data Usage

The collected data is used solely for:

  • Analyzing educational institutions in your professional network
  • Creating visualizations of education patterns
  • Generating insights about academic trends in your network

4. Data Storage

We do not store any personal data permanently. All data is processed temporarily during the analysis session and is not retained after the session ends.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. The analysis results are only visible to you.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

7. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy or your data, please contact us through our GitHub repository at: https://github.com/Ksambaa/samscrap-app

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
